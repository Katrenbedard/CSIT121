Notable Films

The House of the Seven Gables(1940)

The House of the Seven Gables, directed by Joy May, is a film adaptation of the 1851 novel of the same name. It is also a remake of the 1910 film adaptation. Price plays the character of Clifford Pyncheon, the eldest brother of the Pyncheon family. Supposedly cursed by 160 years ago by an accussed witch, the Pyncheon's live in fear of the curse coming to enact it's revenge.

Laura(1944)

Directed by Otto Preminger, Vincent Price takes the role of a the fiance of Dara Andrews character Laura. While Price was not the main character, he played a significant role within this film following a detectives investgation of the brutual murder of Laura Hunt. This film holds signfigance as it was chosen in 1999 to be included for preservatino within the Library of Congress' National Film Registry.

Abbott and Costello Meet Frankenstein(1948)

Abbott and Costello hold a significant place in comedic film history and this particular film, directed by Charles Barton, is the first one of their pictures to delve into the horror genre. Price is obviously not the main character, playing as the inconic Invisible Man, but it is one example of him collborating with other historically important celebrties. Like Laura, this film would later be selected in 2001 to be included within the Library of Congress.
