SpyXFamily: A Review

A Spy, An Assassin, and a Telepath: A tale of Family and Deception

Synposis: Twilight, a spy working for the WISE organization, is tasked with infiltrating an elite school as to preserve World Peace. In order to do so, Twilight has to build a faux family and take on the role of Loid Forger, a devoted husband and father. Yor, his fake wife, is an assassain and Anya, his adopted daughter, is a telepath. The three have to maintain their apperances whilst keeping their identities a secret.

Main Character Profile:
  • Twilight: A spy for WISE(Westalian Intelligence), Twilight is a master of disguise and considered the perfect spy. He is tasked with carrying out ‘Operation Strix’ which requires him to enter a fake marriage and adopt a child who can then attend Eden Academy. Under the new name ‘Loid Forger’, Twilight must keep his family together and his daughter in Eden Academy to successfully complete his mission.
  • Yor: A chance encounter with Loid Forger led to her entering a marriage of convenience. Having a family provides her with cover from the Secret Police. During the day she’s an ordinary office clerk at Berlint City Hall, however, at night she is an Assassin known only as Thorn Princess. Like Twilight, she is also unaware of her families’ secret identities.
  • Anya: Adopted by Loid for the purpose of Operation Strix, Anya plays the part of the Forger’s daughter. Unbeknownst to her adoptive parents, Anya was originally a test subject for a mysterious organization that gave her the power of telepathy. She eventually escaped and entered into an orphanage that Twilight would later go to. Despite her past, she is an excitable young girl who wants to help her ‘papa’ achieve world peace. Anya is also the only one who is aware of everyone's true identities.
Story Setting:
  • Westalis: The home country of Twilight, Westalis had once been at war with Ostania that claimed the lives of Twilight and Yor's parents.
  • Ostania: The People's Republic of Ostania is a country on the eastern border of Westalis and is the main setting of the show. Though the war is over with Westalis, tensions are still high and it's up to Twilight to prevent a second war from breaking out.
Story Arch
  1. Found Family: In this arc, Twilight starts off his mission by recruiting Anya and Yor into his family.
  2. Eden Academy: For Anya to attend Eden Academy, she must pass a test and parent involved interview that will judge whether or not she is good enough for the academy's standards. Once accepted, she must also maintain her position in the school by aquiring eight Stella stars and not getting eight tonitus bolts.
  3. Bond and Project Apple: Wanting to get a job for her achievement in maintaining her grades, the Forger family gets mixed up in the plot of Project Apple. Wanting to instill voilence and kick off a second war, Project Apple modified dogs as to have special abilites that would be used to create terrorist attacks. One of those dogs is Bond who has the ability to see the future. The Forger's end up stopping Project Apple and in doing so take Bond as their new pet.
  4. Cruise Arc: After winning a cruise, Anya and Loid end up on the same ship that Yor is working on. While they relax, she is tasked with keeping the wife and child of a dead mob boss safe from other Assassains on board. Over the course of several episodes, the Forger family each play a part in keeping the ship and it's passengers safe.
Review: Though the series is only two seasons in, SpyXFamily is a hit worldwide for a reason. The show balances the comedy of the Forger family with the tragic nature of war perfectly. The protagonists are all balanced characters who, despite having special abilities, also have their own endearing flaws. The pacing for the show keeps interest up without skirting too fast over serious plot points. The inevitable reveal of the Families secrets is still far off but even knowing that I still want to be along for the ride. This is the perfect anime for both new and seasoned watchers and will have a place in my heart long after it’s done.
