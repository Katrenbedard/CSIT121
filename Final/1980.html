Notable Films

Vincent(1982)

In this little known short film directed by Tim Burton, Price plays as the narrator who follows a young boy named Vincent Malloy. Burton, known well by film lovers of all sorts, credits Vincent Price as being his idol and inspiration. Though the runtime is only six minutes, Price referred to Vincent(1982) as "the most gratifying thing that ever happened. It was immortality â€” better than a star on Hollywood Boulevard."

Edward Scissorhands(1990)

Most people will remember Edward Scissorhands (1990) for Johnny Depp and Winona Ryder. However, it is also the last film that Price ever acted in before his death in 1991. His character, The Inventor, creates Edward Scissorhands on a whim and teaches him about the world before his death. The movie is a beautiful send-off for one of the most prolific actors of all time.
